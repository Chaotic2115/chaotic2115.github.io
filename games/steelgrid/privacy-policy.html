Privacy Policy – SteelGrid

Last updated: March 15, 2026

SteelGrid is a free mobile puzzle game developed by Exblox Studio. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Data We Collect

We do not directly collect or store any personal data. However, our app uses third-party services that may collect data as described below.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your consent. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Analytics (GameAnalytics)

We use GameAnalytics to collect anonymous gameplay data to help us improve the game experience. GameAnalytics may collect device information, session data, and gameplay events such as level progression and game actions. All data is anonymized and does not personally identify you. For more information, see GameAnalytics Privacy Policy.

4. Consent (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you will be asked for your consent before personalized ads are shown. You can change your consent at any time in the app settings.

5. Data Retention

Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this data.

6. Children's Privacy

SteelGrid is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

If you have any questions, contact us at: exbloxstudio@gmail.com
